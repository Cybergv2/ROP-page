Riorock Labradors located in New England & East Coast area.
We breed for the Multi-Purpose English Labrador for Quality, Type, Temperament, Soundness and Classic Lab Style having Labradors since 1995.
Our Labs are registered with the American Kennel Club are OFA, CERF, CNM, EIC, Heart Cleared and Optigen tested for PRA.
We actively show our dogs in AKC and International Conformation Shows to earn their Champion Titles.
Riorock is the home to AKC Championship pointed, International and National Champions.
Our puppies make excellent Pets, Family, Therapy/Service Companions and Hunting partners.
We have a limited number of litters and puppies available in Black, Chocolate, and Yellow (Light to Fox Red).
We have proudly donated our labs to Leader Dogs in MI.
We are currently located in the New England/East Coast Area, but our puppies are all over the US.
Please feel free to browse through our website to see photos, pedigrees, history, clearance information on our dogs and puppies.
